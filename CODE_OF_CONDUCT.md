# Code of Conduct

We want a friendly, inclusive community. By participating, you agree to follow these rules.

## Be Respectful
- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent and give helpful feedback.
- Respect differences in background, experience, and opinion.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.

- Threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness a problem, open a GitHub issue and ask for a private channel. We will take reports seriously and respond as soon as we can.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation that violates this code of conduct.
